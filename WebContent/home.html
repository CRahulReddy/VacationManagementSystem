POWERFUL TOOL FOR VACATION MANAGEMENT

Easily track vacations
Cloud-based/Real-Time Solution

Vacation Manager is a simple, user-friendly web-based application with no learning curve. Streamline the entire vacation management process. Real-time notifications of leave requests and approvals or rejections. Avoid manual bookkeeping for employee's leave status. The application features automated calculations of leave balance and accruals.
Employees and managers have a meaningful insight to the data on their dashboards.

How does the seamless process work?
